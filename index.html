Welcome to Black Dragon Karate

Dive into the world of martial arts with Black Dragon Karate, a beacon of excellence in Snowflake, AZ. Led by a seasoned sensei with an impressive 30 years of dedication to the craft, our dojo stands as a testament to true mastery and passion. With a collection of 14 black belts spanning diverse disciplines—including Shorin Ryu, Ativism, Hawaiian Kenpo, and Polynesian Kenpo—our legacy is rich and our commitment unwavering. Whether you're embarking on your first martial arts journey or seeking to elevate your skills, Black Dragon Karate offers an unparalleled experience. Join us, and become a part of a tradition of excellence.
