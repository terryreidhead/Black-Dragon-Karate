Our History

Discover the rich history of Black Dragon Karate and learn about our journey, milestones, and achievements over the years.

1985
Black Dragon Karate was founded by Sensei Jane Doe, marking the beginning of our legacy in martial arts training.
1990
The dojo expanded to accommodate more students, introducing advanced training programs and workshops.
2000
Black Dragon Karate celebrated its 15th anniversary with a grand tournament, witnessing participation from renowned martial artists worldwide.
2015
The dojo introduced specialized training programs for kids, emphasizing discipline, fitness, and self-defense.
2020
Black Dragon Karate celebrated its 35th anniversary, marking over three decades of excellence in martial arts training.
